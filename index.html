About Kumar Aman

Hi! I'm Kumar Aman, a multidisciplinary designer, filmmaker, and the founder of The Minimical, a Design & Branding Studio dedicated to crafting impactful visual narratives that elevate brands and connect them with their audience. With over 6 years of experience and a portfolio of 500+ projects spanning branding, motion design, video production, and digital marketing, I bring a unique blend of creativity and strategy to every project.

Our Philosophy

For me, design is more than just aesthetics — it's a powerful tool to tell stories, build connections, and create impact. I believe in the transformative power of good design and strive to bring that to every project I work on.

Our Studio

Located in the heart of the city, Minimical Studios Co. is our creative playground. This is where ideas take shape, pixels are perfected, and stories are told. Our studio is fully equipped for both digital and film productions — from pre-production planning to post-production magic.
